Data Privacy Policy

Your privacy and data protection rights

Introduction

At MEMBRO, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our home portal services.

Last Updated: December 2024

This policy complies with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) and international data protection standards including GDPR.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number, unit address
  • Account Information: Username, password (encrypted), profile preferences
  • Property Information: Unit details, ownership status, household members
  • Service Usage: Booking history, payment records, support requests

Automatically Collected Information

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, session duration
  • Communication Records: Support tickets, feedback, correspondence

How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Process bookings, manage accounts, provide support
  • Communication: Send notifications, updates, and important announcements
  • Payment Processing: Handle billing, payments, and financial transactions
  • Security: Protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and security threats
  • Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to enhance our services
  • Legal Compliance: Meet regulatory requirements and legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Verified vendors for maintenance, cleaning, and other services
  • Payment Processors: Secure payment gateways for transaction processing
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Emergency Situations: To protect safety and prevent harm

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Transfer your data to another service
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure servers with regular security updates
  • Access controls and authentication systems
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Staff training on data protection practices
